This directory contains car images for offline mode.

Default car images should be placed here with the following filenames:
- mahindra_scorpio.jpg
- suzuki_swift.jpg 
- tata_nexon.jpg
- toyota_fortuner.jpg
- bmw_x5.jpg
- tesla_model3.jpg
- honda_city.jpg

The app will look for these images when online images are not available.
